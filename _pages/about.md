I am a Masters Student in Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Dallas. I currently work on World Models and improving Multimodal Language Models.

I previously worked as a Research Assistant at the [Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore](https://iisc.ac.in/), advised by [Prof. Pradipta Biswas](https://cambum.net/PB/) where I worked on problems related to Computer Vision, focusing on Diffusion Models. In parallel, I worked on Emotion Analysis in colloboration with [Dr. Andrea Kohlase](https://www.hnu.de/andrea-kohlhase) & [Dr. Michael Kohlase](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Kohlhase) from the [University of Applied Sciences Neu-Ulm, Germany](https://www.hnu.de/en/university).

Before this, I led a research project while completing my Bachelor's at [NMIT, Bangalore](https://www.nmit.ac.in/) advised by [Dr. Krishanarao Venkatesh](https://nmit.irins.org/profile/150518), [Dr. Vasanthakumar G U](https://nmit.irins.org/profile/130197) & [Dr. Dileep Reddy Bolla](https://vidwan.inflibnet.ac.in/profile/176361) in colloboration with [Unisys](https://www.unisys.com/).
